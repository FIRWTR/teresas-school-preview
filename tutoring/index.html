One-on-One Tutoring · K–12

For the kids who learn differently — and the parents who won't give up.

Private, one-hour sessions built around your child alone. Dyslexia and ADHD are our specialty — confidence is our product.

The door closes. The phone stays at the desk. For one hour, your child is the only student in the world.

Most of our students are dyslexic. They arrive believing they’re bad at school — and somewhere in these hours, reading finally makes sense. After every single session, a written note lands in your inbox telling you exactly what happened. You’ll never wonder if it’s working.

  • One student · one teacher · one full hour
  • Barton-certified for dyslexia
  • IEP & 504 advocacy included
